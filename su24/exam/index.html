Exam Info

Final

The final will be held in-person on Wednesday, August 7th, 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM PT. The alternate time exam will start at Wednesday, August 7th, 6:00PM PT. Alternate exams will only be offered for documented time conflicts, extenuating circumstances, and religious accommodations.

If you have DSP accommodations or would like to request an adjustment to your exam (such as a left-handed desk, a seat in a specific room, or an alternate exam due to time conflicts), please fill out the final adjustments form by Friday, August 2nd, 11:59 PM PT. We will respond to all requests submitted before this date by Monday, August 5th, 11:59 PM PT.

After this point, we cannot guarantee that we will be able to accommodate any alteration requests, and requests will be reviewed on a rolling basis. However, if you're feeling unwell or there's an emergency, please email cs61c@ directly.

Please only email cs61c@ for emergencies or if you're feeling unwell. Please do not post on Ed, since there's a higher chance of us missing your post.

Room and seat confirmations will be sent by Monday, August 5th, 11:59 PM PT via email.

Alternate and Online Exams

If you have another exam conflict that directly overlaps with the main exam time, we are offering one alternate exam on Wednesday, August 7th, 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM PT.

There will not be an online exam for the final.

Final Scope

The final covers the following topics:

  • Lectures 1 through 24
  • Discussions 1 through 12
  • Labs 0 through 9
  • Homeworks 1 through 7
  • Projects 1 through 4

Materials only covered in the textbook are not within scope.

Final Resources

The exam is closed-book, closed-note, closed-internet, with the following exceptions:

  • You can prepare and use up to 2 two-sided letter-sized study sheets for the final. You can reuse your midterm study sheet for the final.
  • Study sheets must be handwritten. Digitally handwritten notes may be printed and used.
    • We will take away any study sheets that do not follow this policy.
  • On the exam, you will be provided with the 61C reference card.

Midterm

After this point, we cannot guarantee that we will be able to accommodate any alteration requests, and requests will be reviewed on a rolling basis. However, if you're feeling unwell or there's an emergency, please email cs61c@ directly.

Please only email cs61c@ for emergencies or if you're feeling unwell. Please do not post on Ed, since there's a higher chance of us missing your post.

Room and seat confirmations will be sent by Wednesday, July 10th, 11:59 PM PT via email.
